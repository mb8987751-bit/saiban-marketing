His deep expertise, client-first philosophy, and encyclopaedic knowledge of Lahore's property landscape have made Saiban Marketing the go-to name for thousands of satisfied clients.

Complete Real Estate Solutions — Backed by 30+ Years of Expertise

At Saiban Marketing, we offer a comprehensive suite of real estate services tailored to meet the needs of buyers, sellers, investors, and overseas Pakistanis. With more than 30 years of expertise behind every recommendation, you can be confident that our guidance is grounded in real market knowledge.

DHA Lahore File Trading

DHA file trading is one of Lahore's most active real estate investment segments. Saiban Marketing provides verified, up-to-date rates for DHA files across all phases — residential and commercial — giving buyers and sellers the accurate data they need. We handle complete documentation and transfer formalities.

Residential Property Sales & Purchase

Whether you are searching for a 5 Marla home in DHA Phase 5 or a 2 Kanal plot in DHA Phase 6, our residential property team delivers expert guidance backed by real market experience. We help buyers find the right property at the right price and sellers achieve maximum value.

Commercial Property Consultancy

Saiban Marketing helps investors and business owners identify premium commercial real estate opportunities across Lahore's most desirable locations. From DHA commercial files and plots to office spaces, we provide data-driven recommendations aligned with your investment goals.

New Project Marketing & Consultancy

We work closely with leading developers to give our clients early and exclusive access to new housing schemes and mixed-use developments. Our team evaluates every project for viability before recommending it, ensuring your investment is in safe hands.

Overseas Pakistani Investment Services

Investing in Pakistani real estate from abroad can be complex. Saiban Marketing simplifies the entire process for overseas Pakistanis — from project selection and documentation to fund transfer guidance and post-purchase management.

Property Management

Owning property is one thing; managing it is another. Our property management service takes care of everything from tenant sourcing and rent collection to maintenance coordination and documentation renewal — freeing you to enjoy the returns on your investment.
